Simcad SamPOS 2017

Version 1.0 ( 3 March 2017)
Simcad`s SamPOS system is an optical retail point of sales and stock control system that designed to help the opticians or optometrist practices to manage and maximise the profit of their retail businesses.
Features include
  • Easy to use stock item input interface
  • Automatic barcode generation for new stock items
  • Customer`s purchase and prescription history
  • Comprehensive sales tracking reports to monitor profitability of the business
  • Malaysian GST Compliant (GST summary report and TAX Invoice)
